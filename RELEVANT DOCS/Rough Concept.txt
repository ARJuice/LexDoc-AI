1️⃣ Core Concept of the App
Your system is not just a document storage system.
It is:
AI-powered organizational knowledge platform
Users come here to:
1. Upload internal documents
2. Read AI summaries quickly
3. Search documents across departments
4. Understand key insights
So the UX must prioritize:
Search → Read → Understand NOT Upload → Store → Forget
